About Meetch

Emerging from NYC’s electronic music scene, a city known as a breeding ground for new talent – Meetch was first discovered on Soundcloud and subsequently asked to play a series of shows, including the renowned Kahbang Festival in Bangor Maine. Soon amassing a loyal fanbase, performances followed at legendary NY nightspots Pacha, Stage 48 and Club Amazura – along with Meetch sharing stages with the likes of Skrillex, Deorro, 12th Planet and Paper Diamond. Fast forward to the present and with a decade of live performances under his belt, Meetch now calls the musical melting pot of LA his home and has a dialled in focus on House music – which is underpinned by his early years spent in NYC and playing parties across the country.
